About Monster Survivors

**Monster Survivors** is an action-packed roguelike survival game where you face off against increasingly challenging waves of monsters. Armed with unique abilities and weapons, your goal is simple yet challenging: survive as long as possible while defeating hordes of enemies.

Each playthrough offers a unique experience with randomized power-ups, diverse enemy types, and strategic choices that shape your survival strategy. Whether you prefer close combat or ranged attacks, Monster Survivors provides endless replayability and excitement.

The game features intuitive controls perfect for both **desktop and mobile devices**, ensuring you can enjoy the monster-slaying action anywhere, anytime. With its addictive gameplay loop and progressive difficulty, Monster Survivors will keep you coming back for "just one more run!"
